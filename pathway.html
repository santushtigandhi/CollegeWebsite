About Pathway: Career Counseling

Members of Placement Cell

Pathway: Career Counselling and Placement Cell Placement is the buzz word among students of the present generation. Colleges are no longer making a name on the top listbecause of the quality education they impart but also on providing best placements. Pathway, Career counselling and placement cell of Ram Lal Anand College works on a threefold manner to prepare our students right from the day one to face the global requirements. It is a forum, managed by a team of students and guided by faculty, which brings the best of companies for recruitments as well as internships to the college.

Career Counselling and Personality Grooming Programs: Pathway conducts series of seminars and workshops at a regular basis with the purpose of preparing and keeping the students of the college abreast of various developments in the corporate world through the Personality development program which gives them one-to one interactive sessions on programs ranging fromgroup discussion, personal interview, resume writing, soft skill developmentetc. in the beginning of the session for proper grooming and direction.Recently, Abbscissa HR Consulting agency was hired to hold an extensive workshop for our students.Many of our students pursue post-graduation in professional and academics for which students are encouraged and kept well informed aboutcareer counselling regarding higher education.

Internships:Summer internships are working as a bridge to facilitate students to get better placements. Students are well informed through emails and social media regarding opportunities available in the corporate world. Many students are selected for the internships like Akaaro consulting and training, Crisil, Nestkeys, Urbanclap, ICDL,CNFC etc. and gain experience along with pocket money. The students are also encouraged to participate in various other competitive activities like ICDL Digital challenge, Young India Challenge, Dell My PC My Story, Teach India Corporate social responsibility initiative of TOI with British Council, Internshala Career Scholarship for Girls, Internshala VTC Young Achiever Scholarships, CNFC(Times group) on job Training program for journalism students, Internshala Graphic Design Contest for summer internship to name a few.

Placement Cell: Students are kept updated about activities of Central Placement Cell of University of Delhi. Students participate through CPC in various placement drives at university of Delhi. The Placement cell of college steps forward by themselves approaching and maintaining relationships with the HR departments of the corporate organizations. The companies visited and selected students through placement drive areIBM India, Genpact, AON Hewitt, American Express, CoCubes Technologies, ICDL, Akaaro consulting and training, Skreenin Interactive Delhi, Pure Research, Urbanclap etc. Pathway works in close association of alumni of our college, who are well paced in all walks of life. They also keep us apprised of any internship/job prospects in their company.We try to bring our alumni and the current students on common platforms, where they can share their knowledge, experience and current demand of the market and buildinga healthy relationship.
